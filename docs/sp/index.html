Brand Guidelines

Logo

Our new logo goes back to our roots. Back to the silicon board our company started with.
As it expands and collapses between the full and condensed logo, it changes size with the amount of data contained within it, just like our products!

Full Logo

This is our brand signature. It’s the first thing you see on the landing page of the website, on the packaging, etc.

Condensed Logo

This will be used much more than the full logo. As soon as you scroll on the website, the full logo reduces down to SP. It is seen as a lockup alongside product names, on social media, on ID cards - everything SP.

Graphic Elements

Brand Continuity

Whether you’re looking at a webpage, product packaging, or an ID card, all the icons, buttons and page furniture link back to the logo in their composition.

Imagery

Style

A clean, welcoming photography style that embodies our three keywords: expert, reliable and human-centric.

Product Photography

Product photography should have full focus on the product itself, with few distractions surrounding it. A simple textured/coloured background that compliments the product and its concept.

Lifestyle Photography

Lifestyle photography should include people where possible, using the product and representing the demographic we’re targeting.
